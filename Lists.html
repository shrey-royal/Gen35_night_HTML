How to make a Cake:

  • Get the ingredients.
  • Mix them together.
  • Bake the cake.

How to drive a car:

  • Start the engine.
  • Put the car into gear.
  • Check your mirrors.
  • Release the handbrake.
  • Press the accelerator pedal gently.
  • Release the clutch slowly.
  • Turn the steering wheel to the left or right to go in that direction.

How to make a cup of tea:

  • Boil some water.
  • Put a tea bag in a cup.
  • Pour the water into the cup.
  • Wait for 2 minutes.
  • Remove the tea bag.
  • Add some milk.
  • Add some sugar.
  • Stir it.
