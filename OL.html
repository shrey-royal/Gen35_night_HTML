Fruit Salad Recipe

  1. Wash the fruit

    Thouroughly wash the fruits under cold water to remove any dirt or residues.

  2. Cut the fruit

    Peel and cut the fruits into bite-sized pieces. Use a sharp knife and a cutting board for safety.

  3. Mix the ingredients

    In a large bowl, combine the cut fruits and gently toss them together to mix well.

  4. Add Sweetener (optional)

    If desired, add a natural sweetener like honey or maple syrup and mix it into the fruit salad

  5. Serve and Enjoy

    Transfer the fruit salad to individual serving bowls and enjoy his refreshing treat!
